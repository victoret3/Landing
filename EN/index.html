FAQ

We have collected and concisely answered the most common queries to give you a more efficient search experience. If you do not find the answer you are looking for, do not hesitate to contact us and we will be happy to help you.

What is a tokenization platform?

A tokenization platform is a technological solution that allows promoters to convert economic rights or interests into digital tokens. These tokens can represent a variety of assets, such as real estate, company shares, works of art, among others. All management is carried out from the tokenization platform, from the creation of tokens to the distribution of dividends.

Why should you use a tokenization platform?

On the one hand, it will open a new way of financing, it will increase the liquidity of your assets and it will improve the transparency of your operations. Furthermore, tokenization can help you with the global expansion of your business by removing geographical barriers. On the other hand, it will help you eliminate human errors and optimize your management tasks.

How does the ATYUM tokenization platform work?

Our tokenization platform has all the management processes automated and is very intuitive and easy to use. It does not require prior knowledge of blockchain or tokenization. The best thing is to request a demo and see it yourself.

What is tokenization and how can it benefit my business?

Tokenization is the process of converting a right or economic interest, such as a participating loan or cash flows, into digital tokens. Through our tokenization platform, we help you connect with a global network of investors willing to support your business by purchasing these tokens. In addition, tokenization allows you to efficiently and transparently manage various aspects, such as the registration of token owners and the distribution of dividends. With tokenization, you can focus on what you do best: growing your business.

Do you need prior knowledge to start investing?

No. At ATYUM, we believe in accessibility and want to make it easy for all investors to participate, regardless of their experience with blockchain or cryptocurrencies. To do this, we have integrated into our tokenization platform a digital wallet solution that is easy to use and that allows investors to participate in tokenization projects without the need to be technological experts. Investors can register an account on the platform and they will be provided with a digital wallet. Through this wallet, investors can buy, sell and store tokens in a safe and easy way. In addition, to further facilitate the investment process, we have incorporated fiat payment gateways, which means that investors can participate in tokenization projects using traditional currencies.

How does ATYUM ensure legal compliance of tokenized projects?

We prioritize legality and transparency. We work with experts who draft the necessary documentation for each project, including an exhaustive whitepaper or investment document. This document includes a detailed analysis of the project, a study of the financial structure, and a detailed identification and mitigation of risks. All this guarantees that our projects are in compliance with the current regulations of the National Securities Market Commission (CNMV). This rigorous legal process ensures the legitimacy of each project listed on the tokenization platform and promotes trust in both token issuers and investors.

How does the distribution of dividends work?

At ATYUM, we have simplified the distribution of dividends using blockchain technology. When the time comes to distribute the dividends, you simply have to deposit the funds in USDT (a type of cryptocurrency) in a smart contract from the administration panel of the tokenization platform. Investors, in turn, will be able to claim these dividends directly from the app, simply by pressing a button. It is an intuitive and simple process that facilitates the distribution of dividends for both companies and investors.

What other services does ATYUM offer to support my project?

ATYUM is much more than a simple tokenization platform, we strive to offer you full support to ensure the success of your project. As part of our additional services, we can design and develop a marketing strategy tailored to your specific needs. This strategic approach can help you attract the right investors for your project and maximize your exposure. Additionally, we can develop a custom website for your project. This page acts as an extension of your online presence, providing a space for potential investors to find out more about your project. Here, you can highlight things like your team, experience, tokenization details, and much more.
